Group trips fall apart in group chats — scattered plans, forgotten expenses, photos stuck on different phones. Triovel gives travel groups a shared timeline, a place to capture moments together, and expense splitting that handles multiple currencies. Everything works offline first, so nothing is lost on a mountain or in a subway — data syncs automatically when connectivity returns.
